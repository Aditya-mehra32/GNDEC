Introduction

Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College (GNDEC), established in 1956 in Ludhiana, Punjab, is one of the oldest and most prestigious engineering institutions in northern India. It was set up under the aegis of the Nankana Sahib Education Trust to promote technical education among the rural and underprivileged communities, especially the Sikh minority. The college is affiliated with I.K. Gujral Punjab Technical University and approved by AICTE.

  • Location: Ludhiana, Punjab, India
  • Campus: Urban, spread over 150 acres
  • Accreditation: National Board of Accreditation (NBA)
  • Programs Offered: Undergraduate, Postgraduate, and Doctoral programs in various engineering disciplines
  • Research Focus: Emphasis on applied research and innovation in engineering and technology
  • Campus Facilities: Modern classrooms, laboratories, libraries, hostels, sports facilities, and student activity centers
  • Cultural Diversity: A vibrant campus life with students from various states and countries
